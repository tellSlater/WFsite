About

Ceation

I decided to create this site in order to have a place where I can gather all my projects, document them properly and share them with you. I enjoy creating and sharing - I treasure the feeling that I get when I see others enjoy my work, learn from my work or being entertained by it. I may also have the opportunity to share some of the projects of a few close friends.

You may ask "why not make a profile on Instructables or any other such website?". The answer is that I wanted to try and take on the challenge of building a website on my own. In the process, I have come to realize that making a profile and using it on a platform is one thing but making your own website is quite another. I could compare the experience as living in a hotel room (a platform) or living in your own house (own website). No matter how many utilities and comforts a big hotel may have, it will never be as cosy as your own space. Thus, this site was born!

Content

The content on this site falls in two main categories in the home page: PROJECTS and MUSIC. On the PROJECTS page there is content mainly about crafting-themed projects that revolve around 3d designing, 3d printing, electronics, synthesizers, CNC and more. On the MUSIC page there is content about songs that I write, improvise, cover or interpret alone or with friends. There is also a link to a contact form on the main page - feel free to ask me anything you like or just send me a message!

Difficulties

I decided to categorize the projects according to their difficulty in three categories: EASY, NORMAL and HARD. Don't expect this to be an accurate estimation and, moreover, don't let the titled difficulty of a project disheart you. I am sure that anyone can tackle any project if he/she really wants to do it. Difficulties are only a broad line I drew to let you know of the amount of effort I had to put into each project and the possible amount of effort a project could demand from you as well.
