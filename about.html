About

Creation

I enjoy creating and sharing - I treasure the feeling that I get when I see others enjoy my work, learn from my work or being entertained by it. I decided to create this site in order to have a place where I can gather all my projects, document them properly and share them with you. I may also have the oprotunity to share some of the projects of a few close friends.

You may ask "why not make a profile on instructables or any other such website?". The answer is that I wanted to try and take on the challenge of building a website on my own - using HTML and CSS. In the process, I have come to realize that making a profile and using it on a platform is one thing but making your own website is quite another - there is more freedom to it and it feels different. I could compare the experience as living in a hotel room (a platform) or living in your own house (own website). No matter how many utilities and comforts a big hotel may have, it will never be as cosy as your own room. Thus, this site was born!

Content

The content on this site falls in two main categories in the home page: projects and music. On the projects page there is content mainly about crafting-themed projects that revolve around 3d designing, 3d printing, electornics, synthesizers, CNC and more. On the music page there is content about songs that I write, improvise, cover or interprete alone or with friends. There is also a link to a contact form on the main page - feel free to ask me anything you like or just send me a message!

My background

I studied classical and electric guitar in my younger years and have acquired the RGT grade 7 diploma for electric guitar. I graduated from the “Lycée Léonin de Patissia Athènes” high-school. Finally, I received a Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Thessaly. Other than my “legitimately proven knowledge” that is stated in the previous three sentences, I have been developing other skills on my own as well. This world has so many intriguing subjects in it that every month I catch myself trying to learn or create something new (learning HTML and CSS and creating this site for instance).
