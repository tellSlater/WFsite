Pico planter

Summary

In this project, I turn a simple self hydrating pot into a "smart" pot, featuring:

  1. Lots of sensors:
    • Temperature
    • Humidity
    • Light
    • Soil moisture
    • Water tank level
  2. Water pump
  3. Water tank level alarm
  4. Local data logging
  5. Cloud data logging

The reason quotes were used for "smart" is that this was mostly a way to learn new things for me. All of these sensors and logging is not necessary to automate watering plants. The pump alone operating on a timer is sufficient. This page is a work in progress.
