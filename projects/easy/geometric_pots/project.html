Geometric pots

Summary

This was a craft of the moment. These cover-pots are designed to take 54mm diameter flower pots. They are easy to 3D print and require a minimum amount of filament. In this page you will find the STL files for these pots and my preferred setting for 3D printing.

Specifications

This ia a design for a cover-pot for standardized small flower pots. I bought three small pots from the mall for about 4€. These pots have a standard top diameter of about 54mm. I used Fusion360 for this design. I made sure that the pots would snugly fit and kept the overhangs at a maximum of 55°.

3D printing

This design is to be 3D printed using the "spiralize outer contour" setting, so no supports required. I suggest using a line width of 0.6mm. I had a good fit and a good print using this setting. Given that a medium printing speed is used, all three pots should print in less than 2.5 hours.
