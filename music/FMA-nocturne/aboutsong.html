FMA Nocturne of Amestris (cover)

Creation

Full Metal Alchemist is a splendid anime and on top of that (in my opinion) has one of the best soundtracks out there. The music of this anime was composed, scored, and arranged by Akira Senju. It was then executed by the Warsaw Philharmonic Orchestra & Choir. I have listened to that soundtrack a lot and decided to interpret this particular song for guitar:

Content

Whenever I write a duet I pan the instruments left and right completely so that the listener will have the opportunity to isolate them if they want to. I have interpreted the melodies for a guitar duet. You can watch an execution of my arrangement here:
