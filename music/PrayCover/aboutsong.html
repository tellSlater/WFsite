Pray (cover)

Creation

This is a song from highschool(2010). "Pray" from "Blessthefall". I remember I really felt that song back then.

This was my first try in mixing a whole band. I used Wavelab to mix this song - didn't know any better. I wrote and imported the drums from Guitar Pro. Pretty lame sound, I know, but this mix was (more or less) my first child and I was proud of it!

Content

I tried to be as close to the original song as possible. Two guitars, bass and drums. I was actually quite bad at screaming but I used guitar overdrive over my voice to help bring out some noise.
