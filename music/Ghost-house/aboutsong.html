Ghost house theme (cover)

Creation

This is another example of an 8bit song I liked, so I decided to make a small cover of it. It is such a sweet little melody! It plays in "ZELDA - Link's awakening". In the story you help a nostalgic ghost visit its old house. This is the song that plays once you enter. Finally, you guide the ghost to its grave where it can find peace.

I used a recorder to play the melody and an e.guitar for the rhythmic part. The sound is quite bad because all I had to work with back then was a webcam's microphone! (December 2017)
