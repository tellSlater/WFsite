Music

15 fathoms, counting (cover)

Creation

I wanted to attempt to make a YouTube video covering a full band song. BMTH's "15 fathoms, counting" was simple enough for me to be able to play all its parts so I gave it a try. The feeling this song brings out is incredible to me. Especially since it stands next to songs like "pray for plagues" or "medusa" in the album.

Content

There are two guitars(one lead - one rhythmic), drums and bass. That was also very convenient because I would have to split the screen in four parts if I wanted to make it into a video. I mixed the song in Cubase and made a video on top of it.
