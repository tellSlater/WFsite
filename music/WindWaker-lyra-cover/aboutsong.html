Wind Waker (cover)

Creation

Back in July 2015 I made a friend who could play the lyra. I finally met someone who could play some strings and so I wanted to make something relevant. For some time I admired many of the covers I have seen on the Wind Waker theme, so I wanted to make one of my own! Also, it is quite funny seeing how much similar playing the lyra is with Makar (leaf-musician from Wind Waker) playing his violin.

Content

First of all, the original form of this cover was this video that we made:

The main instruments we used were a guitar, a cretan lyra, a recorder, an ocarina, some VST pads and bass and we also used a bucket and a pan for percussions! I mixed this in Cubase
