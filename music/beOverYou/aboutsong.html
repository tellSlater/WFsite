I'll be over you (cover)

Creation

This is a break up song and the reason why I ended up doing a cover on it was... well... a break up! It was my way of coping with it - expressing myself through a song. This guitar arrangement in particular was created by Andy McKee - a famous acoustic guitarist. The original song belongs to Toto and was released in 1986 in their album Fahrenheit.

Content

It is me playing Andy's arrangement of this song on a Fender acoustic guitar.
