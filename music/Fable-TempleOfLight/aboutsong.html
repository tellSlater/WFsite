Temple of Light (cover)

Creation

I have never played Fable, but when I heard this song on YouTube I immediately fell in love with it:

At that time (November 2014) I was trying to learn a bit of keys. My family had a KORG M-1 that I regularly played. When I heard the Temple of Light theme it seemed easy enough to try interpreting it for piano - so I did just that! Here is a video of my arrangement:

Content

This is an arrangement I made of the Temple of Light theme from Fable. It is performed on a KORG M-1 keyboard. I also added some effects on the keyboard's sound to get its sound closer to the original track.
