Pios ine aftos

Creation

Me and Ioanna were messing around with this chord progression while I started singing these lyrics over it. It was surprisingly quite fitting and we decided to record it for fun! The lyrics are from the song "Ποιός είναι αυτός" from the artist Pantelis Pantelidis - famous in Greece. You can listen to the original song on YouTube for reference:

Content

Firstly, I wrote the chord progression. Then, I had Ioanna do 3 or 4 iterations of practicing in order to write a lead part. I actually lied to her! I was recording her practicing the lead part and used that in the mix. I cut the best parts of the solo and mixed it in the song. Sometimes the feeling of being recorded can make it harder to perform so I pulled this old trick on her! We added some drums and pads but never finalized it.
