Harmonics

Creation

I have always liked the sound of harmonics on my father's Fender Stratocaster. Around January 2020 I wrote this harmonics riff using that guitar and decided to turn it into a full song.

Content

The song revolves around that harmonics riff, thus I named the song Harmonics. The riff is repeated throughout the song until its very end. I added another more bass guitar part to give some volume to the song and also added two melodic parts. I used some pads too and a pickup static effect in the background.
