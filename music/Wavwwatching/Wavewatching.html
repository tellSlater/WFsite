Wavewatching

Creation

This is a song I wrote while experimenting for the first time with a synth I wrote for an AVR microcontroller. I called it Znear Synth! I more or less wanted to test its capabilities. The synth itself is available as a project in the site's projects page. (Link here)

Content

Every sound audible in this song is a result of additive synthesis using this synthesizer. In other words - all the sounds are fake! The ocean, the air that blows, the seagulls, the pads, the bells and the bass. The sea waves and air are made of a combination of noise waves whose volumes are modulated by ADSR envelopes. The seagulls are high pitched - swept down triangle waves with short attacks and longer releases.

I should point out that the synth is incapable of outputting more than two waves at a time. Whenever more than two sounds are audible in this song it is because they were mixed together after pre-recorded as samples from the synthesizer. I wrote this song using Reaper.
