ZELDA Overworld guitar trio cover

Creation

I have always liked The Legend Of Zelda in general and especially these 8 bit games like Link's awakening. I admire the fact that the musicians could produce such nice tunes for these games using the limited resources that the old gaming consoles had. Listening to this specific tune while playing the game, I always had on my mind that it could be interpreted for guitar. So, I just went ahead and did just that - I interpreted the 3 melodies of this song for an electric guitar trio. You can listen to the original tune here:

Content

What you hear in this cover are 3 guitars each playing a melody of this tune: one guitar plays the melody, a second guitar plays secondary melodies and, finally, a guitar for the bass. In order to get a better idea you can watch this video:
